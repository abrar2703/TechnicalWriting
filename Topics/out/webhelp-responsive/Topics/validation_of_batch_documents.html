Validation of Batch Documents

Steps followed in Validation of Batch document are:

Step Action
1 On entering the coating room for a production run, both operators will review batch documents, make sure that all C/D/L parameters are set according to the batch documents provided by the production supervisor.
2 Both operators will verify that the materials and process to determine which side (In or Out) of the Web Release liner are correct and clear then both operators will sign the batch documents so that both operators assume responsibility for the accuracy of all information.
3 If there are any discrepancies, the operators will not proceed with the batch, and the discrepancies will be reported to the production supervisor immediately.
Note: The explanation above is based on our experience with typical products and typical materials.
